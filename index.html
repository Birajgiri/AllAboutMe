My Story

I was born and raised in Kathmandu, Nepal, a beautiful city nestled in the Himalayas, rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. Growing up surrounded by ancient temples and modern innovation sparked my passion for both creative expression and technology.

Currently pursuing my Computer Programming degree at Sault College in Toronto, I'm passionate about web development and software engineering. Beyond coding, I find inspiration in art masterpieces from Van Gogh to Monet, and in music that spans from Frank Ocean's introspective R&B to Tyler the Creator's creative genius.

My journey is defined by the concept of sonder - the profound realization that everyone around us has their own complex, vivid life story. This perspective fuels my creativity in programming, my appreciation for artistic expression, and my love for diverse musical narratives.
