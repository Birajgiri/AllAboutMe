My Favorite Artists

The artists who have shaped my musical taste, featuring my favorite songs from each

Brent Faiyaz

R&B/Soul 2014-Present Maryland, USA

Brent Faiyaz is a contemporary R&B artist known for his smooth vocals and introspective lyrics. His ability to blend modern R&B with elements of alternative soul creates a unique sound that resonates with authenticity and emotional depth.

My Favorite Songs:

  1. L.A.
  2. Poison
  3. Gang Over Luv

Frank Ocean

Alternative R&B 2005-Present California, USA

Frank Ocean is a visionary artist who redefined modern R&B with his innovative approach to songwriting and production. His introspective lyrics and genre-blending soundscapes have made him one of the most influential artists of his generation.

My Favorite Songs:

  1. White Ferrari
  2. American Wedding
  3. Moon River

Tyler the Creator

Hip-Hop/Alternative 2007-Present California, USA

Tyler the Creator is a multifaceted artist known for his creative evolution from provocative rap to genre-defying music. His production skills, unique vocal style, and artistic vision have made him a leading figure in contemporary hip-hop and alternative music.

My Favorite Songs:

  1. Are We Still Friends
  2. Like Him
  3. She

Daniel Caesar

R&B/Soul 2014-Present Ontario, Canada

Daniel Caesar is a Canadian R&B singer known for his soulful voice and gospel-influenced sound. His music combines classic R&B elements with contemporary production, creating timeless songs that speak to love, spirituality, and human connection.

My Favorite Songs:

  1. Death & Taxes
  2. Neu Roses
  3. Superpowers

Sade

Smooth Jazz/Soul 1982-Present United Kingdom

Sade is an iconic British-Nigerian singer whose sophisticated blend of soul, jazz, and R&B has created timeless music. Her smooth vocals and elegant compositions have influenced generations of artists and continue to captivate listeners worldwide.

My Favorite Songs:

  1. Pearls
  2. Kiss of Life
  3. Like a Tattoo
